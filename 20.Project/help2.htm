Dungeon Raid
-----------------------------------------------------
- Trace through paths of similar tiles to defeat monsters and collect
treasures and shields.
- The paths you trace has to go through similar tiles. Shields selected
must have to go through other shileds ect. The only exception is skulls
go through with swords.
- Pressing the Space Bar will cancel a current path if you make a
mistake.
- You may only trace tiles that are one space from the currently traced
path.
- The minimum number of tiles required to complete a path is Three.
- Money earned will go towards the two upgrades below the tiles.
Click to buy.
