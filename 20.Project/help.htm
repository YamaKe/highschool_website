Maplestory
-----------------------------------------------------
- Move with the classic WASD keys for Up, Left, Down, and Right
respectively.
- F is the attack key, you will attack a target in front of you.
- Jumping with the W key can only be done on platforms.
- Pressing the down S key will drop you down one floor.
- At level 10, you will be able to collect the sword at the top of the
map and choose to either continue fighting in the map or moving on
to the boss level.
- On the top floor, do not hold the W key. DO NOT HOLD THE
W KEY.
