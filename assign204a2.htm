Review and double-check everything you are about to post. People often make the mistake of posting things too rashly.
Stop and review. Make sure that the content you are about to post online doesn't offend anyone, reveal personal details, or show yourself as a bad person.

This may sound redundant, but it is essential to a good digital profile!
